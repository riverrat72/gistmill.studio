What We Do

The Gist Mill is a small, independent engineering studio focused on creating practical, high-performance tools for physical therapy and healthcare workflows. We build software that cuts through noise, automates complexity, and lets clinicians spend more time on real patient care.

Our work is currently focused on making a robust digital clipboard that uses digital handwriting recognition through a stylus-based charting interface, future projects include speech-to-text systems, a potential speech "prosthesis" that infers meaning through tone and other auditory communication beyond words — all designed for real clinic environments.

Our Projects

Clipboard

A stylus-first documentation interface for fast, structured charting at the point of care.

Charteyes

A handwriting-to-text model built for real PT note-taking styles, abbreviations, and shorthand.

Chartears

A speech-to-text engine designed for noisy clinic environments and natural speech.
